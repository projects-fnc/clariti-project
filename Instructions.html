Patient Instructions

Come Prepared, Stay Comfortable

Follow these simple guidelines to ensure a smooth and stress-free diagnostic experience.

MRI Scan

Before Your Appointment

  • Arrival: Please reach 20-30 minutes early for preparation. If oral contrast is needed, arrive 45 minutes early (you will be informed during booking).
  • Pacemakers & Implants: Inform us if you have pacemakers, valves, ICDs, pumps, stents, clips, or metal implants. Some devices are not MRI-safe.
  • Claustrophobia: Our wide-bore MRI, calming lights, music, and continuous communication help reduce anxiety. A companion can stay inside for support.
  • Clothing: Wear loose and comfortable clothes. All metal items (jewellery, phone, belts, wallet, cards) must be removed.

Ultrasound

Preparation

  • Abdominal & Pelvic Scans: Drink 3-4 glasses of water 1 hour before the scan and avoid urinating until after the scan.
  • Fasting: No fasting needed unless specifically instructed by your doctor.
  • Clothing: Wear loose, two-piece clothing for easy access to the examination area.

Fetal Scans

For Expecting Mothers

  • Referral: Bring your doctor's referral and any previous scan reports.
  • Timing: Best done between 12-22 weeks depending on scan type.
  • Hydration: Drink water as advised for better image clarity.
  • Comfort: You may bring a companion for support during the scan.

Laboratory Services

Blood & Urine Tests

  • Fasting: 8-10 hours fasting required for glucose, lipid profile, and certain biochemistry tests.
  • Hydration: Drink plenty of water unless fasting is required.
  • Medications: Continue regular medications unless advised otherwise by your doctor.
  • Reports: Digital reports will be shared via WhatsApp/Email within 4-6 hours for routine tests.

NCS & EEG

Nerve Conduction Studies

  • Avoid applying lotions or oils on the skin on the day of the test.
  • Inform the technician if you have a pacemaker or are on blood thinners.
  • Wear loose, comfortable clothing.

Electroencephalogram (EEG)

  • Wash your hair before the test. Do not apply oil, conditioner, or hair spray.
  • For sleep-deprived EEG, follow specific instructions provided at booking.
  • Continue regular medications unless advised otherwise.

Important Note

If you have any specific concerns or medical conditions, please inform our staff at the time of booking. We're here to ensure your diagnostic experience is as comfortable and accurate as possible.
